Project Purpose & Goal

What is the one source of acquired truths for knowlede workers and organizations?

How much time is lost in the searching of retained information, its acquisition and distribution for individuals and organizations that could be otherwise be employed in the creation of value?

OMNI, seeks to reduce the time lost and give back it's users time through a single source of truth that is intuitive to use and manage. Additionally, for organizations and teams, ways of distributing information and managing access and roles to ensure that the their people have access to information when needed and also roles of interacting or modifying that information.

Web Stack & Explanation

OMNI as an individually driven project that requires rapid prototyping, Tailwind CSS is the perfect tool to aid conversion of mockups to live views. Using the class driven styling, time and effort required significantly scaled down in comparison to traditional CSS.

Nest JS & Angular share design patterns and choices ranging from Dependency Injection, modules to structure and programming language choice(Typescript). This removes the overhead of context swithing which can be costly when managing different responsibilities.

MongoDB was a perfect choice for future feature implementations and platform support. With its Realm Sync feature, future implementations of the application on mobile will be able to support offline access and sync thereby ensuring that knowledge and information can be accessed, updated and created offline. In as much as we love the internet and always-available-access, why leave knowledge access to chance?
